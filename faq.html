FAQ

How long does delivery take? +
All orders are dispatched within 1-2 working days. Once dispatched, please allow 3-5 working days for delivery within the UK.
Can I return my order? +
Yes. We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unclean, and free from any scent. Please contact us at info@mymoodcollection.co.uk to initiate a return and we will do our best to accommodate you.
What sizes do you offer? +
We offer sizes XS through to XXL. For the signature MØØD oversized silhouette, we recommend sizing up.
What is the fabric composition? +
All MØØD Collection pieces are made from 70% ring-spun cotton and 30% recycled polyester at 440 GSM for a premium heavyweight feel.
How do I care for my MØØD piece? +
We recommend washing at 30 degrees on a gentle cycle, inside out, to preserve the colour and quality of your garment. Do not tumble dry.
Do you ship internationally? +
We currently ship within the UK. International shipping is coming soon. Sign up to our newsletter to be the first to know when we expand.
I have a question not listed here. How do I get in touch? +
You can reach us at info@mymoodcollection.co.uk and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
