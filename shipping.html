SHIPPING

Delivery Times

All MØØD Collection orders are dispatched within 1-2 working days of your order being placed. Once dispatched, please allow 3-5 working days for delivery within the UK.

Standard UK Delivery: 3-5 working days
Orders are processed Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking information. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking to update after dispatch.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

Important Information

  • Please ensure your delivery address is correct at the time of ordering. MØØD Collection cannot be held responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer.
  • During peak periods, such as new launches or sale events, delivery times may be slightly longer than usual.
  • If your order has not arrived within 7 working days of your dispatch confirmation, please contact us at info@mymoodcollection.co.uk and we will do our best to assist you.

Questions?

If you have any questions about your delivery, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us at info@mymoodcollection.co.uk and we will do our best to help.
