OUR STORY

MØØD Collection was born from a deeply personal place. As someone who has always used clothing as a form of self-expression, I wanted to create something that went beyond aesthetics. Something that spoke to how we actually feel, not just how we want to look.

The idea came during one of life's quieter, more reflective moments. I found myself reaching for certain pieces on certain days, not because they were the most stylish, but because they matched my energy. My mood. And I realised that clothing has the power to hold space for all the versions of us, the motivated, the exhausted, the healing, the thriving.

Each piece in the MØØD Collection is named after a feeling, a state of being, a moment we all recognise. Whether you're Busy Adulting, Mentally Offline, or simply Present Enough, there is a MØØD for that.

Building this brand has been one of the most vulnerable and exciting things I have ever done, especially during the moments where things didn't seem to be going my way.

But those moments are exactly why MØØD exists. To remind you, and me, that every version of you is worth showing up for. The collection is crafted from premium 440 GSM heavyweight fabric, designed for comfort, quality, and longevity. Because you deserve pieces that are built to last, just like you.

Thank you for being here. Thank you for being part of the MØØD.

With love, Debs x
