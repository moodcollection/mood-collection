Our Genesis

MØØD COLLECTION was established with a single vision: to create garments that resonate with the wearer's internal state. What started as a small project in a quiet studio has grown into a community of individuals who value minimalism, quality, and the power of a mood.

Every stitch and every fabric choice is made with intention, ensuring that when you wear MØØD, you aren't just wearing a brand—you're wearing a feeling.
