PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

MØØD Collection operates the website mymoodcollection.co.uk. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order or sign up to our newsletter, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your delivery address and contact number
  • Payment information (processed securely via our payment provider)
  • Your browsing activity on our website

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process and fulfil your orders, send you order confirmation and tracking updates, send you marketing emails if you have opted in, and improve our website and customer experience.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request that we correct or delete your data, and unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and use appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect the functionality of the site.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@mymoodcollection.co.uk.
